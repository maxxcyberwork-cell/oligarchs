About Us

OLIGARCHS is dedicated to attracting and nurturing the talent of gamers by promoting esports as a viable and exciting career path. Our motive is to showcase the numerous opportunities within the esports industry, ensuring that players and teams are well-informed and supported. We strive to provide comprehensive support to esports teams and organizations, helping them grow and succeed in the competitive gaming landscape.

Let OLIGARCHS become a new milestone in the history of competitive gaming. If we all stand together, we can successfully conduct events and elevate our community to the top. We must support and bring together all players, gamers, esports teams, orgs, and creators. With everyone united, we can achieve anything.
