Community Regulations

Rules and guidelines for the OLIGARCHS community

1. General Conduct

All members of the OLIGARCHS community must maintain respectful and sportsmanlike behavior. Harassment, hate speech, cheating, or any form of toxicity will not be tolerated and may result in removal from events and platforms.

2. Eligibility

Participants must meet age and regional requirements as specified for each tournament. Falsifying information will result in disqualification and potential ban.

3. Team & Player Registration

Teams and players must register through official channels. Rosters must be finalized before the deadline. Late changes may be permitted only with organizer approval.

4. Match Conduct

Players must be present at scheduled match times. Disconnects and technical issues should be reported immediately. Organizers reserve the right to pause, replay, or decide outcomes based on fair play.

5. Prizes & Rewards

Prize distribution follows the rules of each event. Winners must provide valid payment details within the stated timeframe. OLIGARCHS is not responsible for tax obligations.

6. Amendments

These regulations may be updated. Continued participation implies acceptance of the current version. Check this page and the Rulebook for the latest rules.
